About ISRO News Portal

Your premier source for Indian Space Research Organisation updates

Our Mission

To provide accurate, timely, and comprehensive coverage of all ISRO activities including satellite launches, space research, and technological advancements in space exploration.

History

Established in 2023, the ISRO News Portal was created to centralize information about India's space program and make it accessible to the public, researchers, and space enthusiasts worldwide.

Objectives

  • Deliver real-time updates on ISRO missions
  • Provide educational resources about space technology
  • Showcase India's achievements in space exploration
  • Promote public engagement with space science
